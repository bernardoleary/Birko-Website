Registrations are now OPEN! Instructions and fee info are below.

SEASON 2022 FEE

• First Kicks / Fun Football U5-U8 (Born 2017 - 2014) - $155
• Juniors U9-U12 (Born 2010 to 2013) - $185
• Youth U13-U17 (Born 2005 to 2009) - $220

IMPORTANT: Please note these registration fees are for season registration only and do not include any of the below:

• Development squad team coaching fees for players in Youth Development Programme or Skill Centre Development squads for Junior grades
• Holiday Programmes
• The 2021 Covid fee credit of $25 for those Junior Section members (aged 5 - 17 years old) who were financial members in 2021.

Fees should be paid online at the time of registration direct to our club bank account or with a valid credit or debit card. If this is not possible for you or you want to arrange a payment plan or other financial assistance, please contact Ross – Juniors Section Convenor at ross@buafc.org.nz to discuss options.

Registrations for all junior and youth players are open until the 15th March 2021, unless the age group is filled prior to that date.

Anyone wishing to cancel their registration prior to the start of the season in April will be offered a refund, less a $25 admin fee, for each player.

HOW TO REGISTER 2022

JUNIORS/YOUTH

Please note if you have changed email address since last registering or have any problem completing the registration form please contact Ross – Juniors Section Convenor at ross@buafc.org.nz to discuss.

Registration open days at the Birkenhead United clubrooms will happen on Sundays 13th, 20th and 27th February between 10am and 1pm. There will be BUAFC merchandise (shorts, socks, shin pads and boots) available for purchase at the open days.

You can register multiple children under one login. You can also add multiple contacts per child to ensure everyone in your family receives information regarding your child.

If you have not previously played for Birkenhead United AFC please detail your previous club and football experience within the registration form.

Once you have completed your registration(s) you will be emailed confirmation of your registration and login details to your membership profile (please check your junk folder). You can then use this new login to make changes or additional registrations.

Please note:

It is the policy of Birkenhead United AFC that all players and club members that use the football grounds and club facilitates are to declare their vaccination status. All players eligible to be vaccinated must declare their vaccination status at registration. We also request that parents and player contacts do the same.

Completing the registration form means you have read, understood and accept all of the below information.

NON-TRIALLED - NRF Community players info:

This is for players who would rather play in more of a social environment with a volunteer coach with training once per week.

Games are played on a Saturday against other local North Harbour and West Auckland based football clubs.

TRIALLED Development Squad Teams and NRFL Youth League Competition player info:

These trials are ONLY for players who wish to play football within the highest ranked teams within these grades and for those committed to two/three trainings per week.

Placement in a Development team is not guaranteed it is by selection invite only following the trials and there are additional training fees associated with these teams. The additional fees will be invoiced if you are selected for a development team.

The U9 to U12 Development teams play on a Saturday.

The U13 to U17 Youth NRFL Development teams will be playing in the Northern Regional Football League (NRFL) Competition which is held on Sundays.

SENIORS

To register as a senior player, you must already be in a BUAFC Senior Team.

If you are not already part of a current mens or womens senior team, please contact Ian at club email - clubcaptain@buafc.org.nz

Adult players in Senior Mens and Womens teams (Born 2005 or earlier) - Team fee per team is paid by team so the player fee is set by that team.

SOCIAL and SUPPORTER MEMBERS

We welcome our valued supporters to join our database so we can communicate with you.

The season fee for Adult non players / social / supporter membership is $70 per person.

The season fee for Pensioners as social /supporters members is $18.50 per person.
